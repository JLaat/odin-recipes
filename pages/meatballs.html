Meatballs

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1-1/3 cups soft bread crumbs
  • 2/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 pounds ground beef

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°.
  2. In a large bowl, combine eggs, water, onion, bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, garlic and seasonings; mix well.
  3. Crumble beef over mixture and mix well.
  4. Shape into 1-1/2-in. balls.
  5. Place meatballs on greased racks in shallow baking pans.
  6. Bake, uncovered, until no longer pink, 15-18 minutes.
